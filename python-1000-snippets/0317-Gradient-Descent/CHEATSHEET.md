## Tips
- Use a small learning rate (e.g., 0.01) to ensure convergence.
- Compute gradients analytically when possible; otherwise use finite differences.
- Stop when the gradient norm is near zero or after a fixed number of iterations.
- Normalize inputs to help convergence.
